What changed from v1

Sound selector moved to overflow menu. Tap the three dots → native iOS context menu with soundscape options, mix levels, session settings, and "The Science" deep link. Not a dropdown pill on the main screen.

Biometrics are bare numbers, not pills. BPM and HRV sit above the wave zone as large monospaced values with tiny labels below. No containers, no borders — they breathe with the visual field. In Energize mode they're larger with a safety status badge.

Less chrome overall. The compact sound+data bar from v1 is gone. The screen is cleaner. The Orb and waves own the space. Controls are at the bottom where iOS users expect them.
