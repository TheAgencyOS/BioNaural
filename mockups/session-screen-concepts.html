Recommendation

Concept 10 (The Hybrid) is the strongest because it follows the design language principle — "earned information" — while giving users contextual access to everything. The Orb is the hero. Data is present but doesn't compete. Sound selection is one tap away. Biometrics are glanceable pills, not a dashboard. The adaptation badge only appears when something happens. And the transport bar includes AirPlay + lock — native iOS controls users expect.

Concept 5 (Energize Command Center) should be the Energize-specific variant — it needs the phase indicator and safety gauge that other modes don't. Concept 8 (Immersive Sleep) should be the Sleep-specific variant — almost nothing on screen, warm tint, auto-dim.
