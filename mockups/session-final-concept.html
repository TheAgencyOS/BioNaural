Design System

One layout, four moods. The three layered waves (ambient/melodic/binaural) are mathematically synced to the live audio — each wave's cycle count matches its actual frequency. The Orb breathes at the beat frequency. Everything you see IS the sound. Sound dropdown and biometric pills stay compact so the Orb owns the screen. Sleep mode fades everything to near-black — circadian-safe. Energize adds the phase bar and HR ceiling gauge — safety-first for the activating mode. AirPlay and screen lock are always accessible in the transport bar.
