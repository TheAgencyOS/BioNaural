The science
Theta→Delta & Sleep
Mechanism

Natural sleep onset follows a theta (4–8 Hz) to delta (0.5–4 Hz) progression. The beat frequency ramps from 6 Hz to 2 Hz over 25 minutes, mirroring this descent.

Adaptive response

When sustained stillness and dropping heart rate indicate sleep onset, the audio begins a slow fade-out. If you stir, the frequency holds steady until you settle.
