## Ratings Strategy

### When to Prompt (3 per year via SKStoreReviewController)

1. **After completing 3rd focus session** — demonstrated engagement, has an opinion
2. **After a personal best streak** — positive emotional moment
3. **After returning from 3+ day absence and completing a session** — re-engagement signals satisfaction

### Never Prompt
- First session, during a session, after crash/error, after paywall interaction, after sub-5-min session

### Credibility Thresholds
- <10 ratings: star rating doesn't display in search
- 50+: minimum credibility
- 200+: competitive in niche
- 500+: strong social proof

## Apple Editorial Feature Pitch

### What Apple Wants to See
1. Design quality — native iOS feel (SF Symbols, Dynamic Type, haptics)
2. Unique differentiator — "adaptive binaural beats" is a clear story
3. Platform integration — HealthKit, Apple Watch, Live Activities, Widgets, Shortcuts, Focus Mode
4. Accessibility — VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, contrast
5. Privacy — "all audio processing happens on-device"

### Pitch Angle
> "BioNaural brings real-time adaptive audio to focus sessions, using on-device processing to generate binaural beats that respond to Apple Watch biometrics. Built with HealthKit for Mindful Minutes, Live Activities for Lock Screen, and Focus Mode integration."

### Submit: developer.apple.com/contact/app-store/promote/
Submit 8+ weeks before target feature date.

### Seasonal Windows

## Description Strategy

First 3 lines (visible before "more" tap) are critical:

> "Your brain adapts. Your focus music should too. BioNaural uses real-time binaural beats and adaptive audio driven by Apple Watch biometrics to help you enter deep focus, study effectively, and work without distraction."

Body format:
1. One-line value prop
2. "WHY BIONEURAL?" — 3-4 benefit bullets
3. "WHAT'S INSIDE" — key features list
4. Social proof (if available)
5. Subscription info (required by Apple, at bottom)

Keep under 4000 chars. Short paragraphs. ALL CAPS section headers (Apple strips markdown).
