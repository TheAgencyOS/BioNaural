# BioNaural — Apple Featuring Strategy

> Apple won't reject you for supporting other wearables. But they'll only FEATURE you if you showcase their ecosystem. Play their game.

---

## The Rule

**Apple's featuring bias is additive, not subtractive.** They don't penalize you for supporting Garmin or WHOOP. But they only feature apps that make Apple's platform look good. Deep Apple Watch + HealthKit + Live Activities + Accessibility = featuring consideration. A wall of competitor logos = invisible to Apple editorial.

---

## The Precedent: Endel

Endel is an adaptive audio app that uses biometrics and Apple Watch. It has been **featured by Apple multiple times** and won design award consideration. BioNaural is in a category Apple's editorial team already cares about.

Other featured health apps that followed this playbook: Gentler Streak (Apple Design Award 2023), Athlytic (Apps We Love), Headspace, Calm (App of the Year 2017).

---

## The Three-Tier Strategy

### Tier 1: Apple-First (Hero — Maximum Featuring)

**Make these the ONLY things in your App Store screenshots and description lead.**

| Apple Feature | Implementation | Featuring Value |
|--------------|---------------|----------------|
| **Apple Watch app** | Standalone watchOS app with session playback, HR display, haptics | Critical |
| **HealthKit (read + write)** | Read HR, HRV, sleep, SpO2. Write mindfulness minutes + workouts. | Critical |
| **Live Activities / Dynamic Island** | Active session progress, HR, timer | High |
| **Widgets (WidgetKit)** | Session streak, recommended mode, quick-start | High |
| **Siri Shortcuts / App Intents** | "Hey Siri, start my focus session" | High |
| **Focus Filters** | Auto-suggest session when Work Focus activates | Medium |
| **Accessibility** | Full VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, Reduce Motion, high contrast | Critical (Apple checks this) |
| **Privacy** | Minimal data collection, on-device processing, clean nutrition label | Critical |
| **StandBy Mode** | StandBy-optimized widget | Medium |
| **Watch Complications** | Next session, current streak, readiness | Medium |

### Tier 2: HealthKit as Silent Universal Adapter

**Users with Oura, WHOOP, Garmin, etc. benefit automatically — without BioNaural mentioning those brands.**

HealthKit reads data regardless of source. When BioNaural reads HRV from HealthKit, it's reading "Apple Health data" — not "Oura data." From Apple's perspective, you're a great HealthKit citizen.

**App Store language:**

DO: *"BioNaural uses Apple Health to personalize your sessions based on heart rate variability, sleep quality, and recovery — no matter which devices contribute to your health data."*

DON'T: *"BioNaural supports Apple Watch, Oura Ring, WHOOP, Garmin, and Fitbit!"*

### Tier 3: Direct Third-Party APIs (Optional/Advanced — NEVER in Screenshots)

- Settings → Connected Services → (Oura, WHOOP, etc.)
- For power users who want readiness scores, recovery scores
- Not in onboarding. Not in screenshots. Below the fold in description.
- Neutral language: *"Connect additional health services"*

App Store description (below the fold only):
> *"For advanced users, BioNaural also connects with popular health platforms for even deeper personalization."*

## Timing for Maximum Feature Probability

| Window | Apple Editorial Theme | BioNaural Angle |
|--------|---------------------|-----------------|
| **WWDC+3 months (Sept)** | "Apps using new iOS/watchOS APIs" | Adopt new HealthKit/Watch APIs same day |
| **January** | "New Year, New You" | "Build a daily focus habit" |
| **May** | **Mental Health Awareness Month** | **Best window.** "Adaptive audio for mental wellness." Pitch by early March. |
| **September** | Back-to-school / Back-to-focus | "Study smarter with adaptive audio" |

**The #1 move:** Adopt new WWDC APIs immediately. If Apple announces a new HealthKit data type or Watch capability at WWDC 2026 (June), have it implemented for the iOS 20 launch (September). Early adopters of new APIs get featuring priority.

## The Bottom Line

1. **Apple won't reject you** for supporting other wearables. Zero risk.
2. **Apple will only feature you** if you lead with their ecosystem. High reward.
3. **HealthKit is your political cover** — silently supports all wearables without mentioning competitors.
4. **Endel proved this works** — adaptive audio + Apple Watch biometrics = a category Apple editorial already cares about.
5. **Every Apple platform feature you adopt** (Watch, Live Activities, Widgets, Shortcuts, Accessibility) increases your featuring probability.
6. **Time your launches** to WWDC adoption cycles and May (Mental Health Awareness Month).
